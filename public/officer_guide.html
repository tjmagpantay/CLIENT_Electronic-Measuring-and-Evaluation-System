📤 How to Submit a Report

  1. Navigate to Submit Report
    Click on "Submit New Report" button from your dashboard or go to "Submit Report" from the menu.
  2. Select Report Type
    Choose the type of report you need to submit. You'll immediately see:
    • The OPR (focal person) for this report
    • The deadline date
    • A link to download the template (if available)
  3. Select Reporting Period
    Choose the month and year for this report submission.
  4. Download Template (Optional)
    If available, click "Download Template" to get the correct format for your report.
  5. Upload Your Files
    Click "Choose Files" and select one or more files:
    • You can upload PDFs, Word documents, Excel sheets, and images
    • Each file must be less than 10MB
    • The system will show a preview of selected files
  6. Add Remarks (Optional)
    Include any notes or comments about your submission.
  7. Submit
    Click "Submit Report" button. You'll see a confirmation message with:
    • Number of files uploaded
    • Submission status (On Time or Late)

📊 Understanding Submission Status

Your submission will have one of these statuses:

On Time

✅ Submitted on or before the deadline. Great job!

Late

⚠️ Submitted after the deadline. Please submit earlier next time.

No Submission

📭 Report not yet submitted. Submit as soon as possible!

💡 Pro Tips

💾 Prepare Files in Advance
Download the template early and prepare your report before the deadline.
🎯 Check OPR Contact
If you're unsure about the report format or content, contact the OPR listed for that report type.
📁 Organize Your Files
Name your files clearly (e.g., "Budget_Jan2026.xlsx") before uploading for easy identification.
⏰ Submit Early
Don't wait until the last minute! Submit a few days before the deadline to avoid last-minute issues.
✓ Double-Check Before Submitting
Review your files and ensure you've selected the correct report type and period.
📱 Keep a Record
After submitting, you can always view your submissions and download the files from "My Submissions" page.
